Revoke Consent & Manage Privacy

This page explains how users can manage privacy preferences and revoke consent for data usage in the Think Fast mobile game.

About Data Consent

Think Fast may display personalized advertisements and collect limited gameplay data to improve the user experience. This may include:

  • Language and app preferences;
  • Game category selections;
  • Anonymous usage and interaction data;
  • Advertising identifiers (where permitted) for analytics and ad personalization.

You can revoke your consent at any time if you no longer want your data to be used for advertising or analytics purposes.

What Happens When You Revoke Consent

  • Personalized ads are disabled (only non-personalized ads will be shown);
  • Analytics used for personalization and advertising are limited;
  • Previously collected advertising identifiers are no longer used for personalization;
  • You can continue playing Think Fast normally, with reduced personalization.

How to Revoke Your Consent

Privacy and consent options are managed exclusively inside the app.

  1. Open the Think Fast app;
  2. Go to the Profile page;
  3. Open the Delete My Account section;
  4. Review the privacy information shown on that screen;
  5. Choose to revoke consent for ads and analytics.

Regional Privacy Notices

  • Users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland may see a GDPR consent form.
  • Users in the United States may see a state-level privacy prompt (such as CCPA/CPRA).
  • In other regions, the app may continue without additional prompts.

Need to Delete Your Account and Data?

Account deletion is permanent and removes your progress, scores, purchases, preferences, and gameplay history from our servers.

Account deletion can be requested directly inside the app via the Profile page.
